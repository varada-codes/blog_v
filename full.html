Rise of the Melodious Technology

Yes! You heard it right. The key to this magic is, AI.


Artificial Intelligence (AI) has revolutionized various industries, including music. With advancements in AI technology, it is now possible to create music using AI-generated voices. AI voices, also known as AI singers or AI-generated vocals, offer a new realm of possibilities for music creators and enthusiasts.

    What are AI voices?

    After a lot of research and experiments, AI voices were generated using various algorithms and deep learning techniques. These algorithms analyse vast amounts of data to learn and mimic the characteristics of human voices, resulting in high-quality, synthesized singing or speaking voices. AI voice generators utilize text-to-speech (TTS) technology to convert written text into vocal tracks that closely resemble human singing or speech. In clearer terms, human voice recordings are used and can be converted to famous singers’ voices using AI music.

    Perks of using AI generated vocals:

  • Versatility:

    It allows artists to explore different vocal styles and genres. AI voices can be customized to suit several musical preferences, be it pop music to 70’s medley. It empowers the musicians to experiment and test various components of music and diverse vocal sounds, which helps them in expanding their creative ideas.

  • Flexibility:

    The most important benefit is that AI voices provide a great flexibility and is way convenient in music production. The general recording sessions require coordination of the singers, presence of the instrumentalists, availability of studio hours and various other challenges. To eliminate these everyday hurdles, AI voices provides artists, the liability to create and produce their music in their terms and at their own pace.

  • Cost effectiveness:

    AI voices save both, time as well as money. For recording some music, vocalists, studio, instrumentalists, etc are needed. And hence, the expenses for them to be assembled and carrying of the heavy instruments, is quite high and is not an easy task. It is also time consuming process. So to avoid all this, AI enables you to focus on the main task; that is music production.

  • Creativity:

    AI voices, serves as a great platform for exploring and experimenting various music techniques. Being an artist, you can try out different vocal styles, genres, and techniques of creating your music without the need of multiple singers, or band or instrumentalists. You can also explore by creating voice transitions from change of male voice to female and vice versa.
